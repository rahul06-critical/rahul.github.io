Festivals

Onam

Onam is the most important festival of Kerala. It is a harvest festival celebrated in honor of the legendary king Mahabali.

  • Pookalam flower decorations
  • Traditional dances
  • Snake boat races
  • Grand feast called Onam Sadya

Vishu

Vishu is the Malayalam New Year festival celebrated in Kerala.

Traditional Dance Forms

Kathakali

A dramatic dance known for colorful costumes and storytelling.

Mohiniyattam

A graceful dance form performed by women.

Theyyam

A ritual dance performed in temples.

Boat Races

Snake boat races called Vallam Kali are famous in Kerala.
